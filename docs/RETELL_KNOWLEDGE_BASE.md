## Products

GitaVox offers two flagship products: VoxFlow and Conversa. These are in-house built solutions designed for high-performance enterprise automation.

### VoxFlow

VoxFlow is "The Human Voice of AI." It is GitaVox's premier voice agent engine.

VoxFlow handles natural, low-latency conversations for:
- Inbound support
- Outbound sales

**VoxFlow features:**
- Low latency (under 800ms)
- Emotional Intelligence
- CRM Integration
- Multilingual

VoxFlow is ideal for call centers, appointment booking, customer support, and sales outreach.

### Conversa

Conversa is "No-Code Intelligence." It is a visual flow builder for creating multi-channel AI assistants.

Conversa deploys to Web, WhatsApp, and Telegram in minutes.

**Conversa features:**
- Visual Flow Editor
- LLM Agnostic
- Real-time Analytics
- Lead Generation

Conversa is ideal for chatbots, lead capture, FAQ automation, and booking flows.
